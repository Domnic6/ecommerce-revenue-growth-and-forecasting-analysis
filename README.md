# Ecommerce Revenue Growth & Forecasting Analysis

This project analyses ecommerce revenue growth trends, Month-over-Month (MoM) and Year-over-Year (YoY) performance, Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR), and 2026 revenue forecasting using the **thelook_ecommerce** Google BigQuery Public Dataset.

The obejctive of this analysis was to calculate key business metrics, perform MoM and YoY growth analysis across the years, evaluate revenue trends, and build forecasting metrics for the upcoming year's revenue and order growth.

---

## Tools Used
- SQL (Google BigQuery)
- Power BI

---

## Analysis Performed
- Built SQL pipelines to transform raw ecommerce transactional data into structured analytical tables.
- Calculated key business metrics including Total Revenue, Total Orders, Average Order Value, and Revenue per Customer
- Performed Month-over-Month (MoM) and Year-over-Year (YoY) growth analysis
- Evaluated long-term revenue trends from 2019-2025
- Built forecasting projections for 2026 revenue and order growth

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue increased from approximately **$0.08M in 2019 to $3.15M in 2025**, indicating rapid early-stage business expansion
- Revenue per customer remained relatively stable, suggesting growth was driven primarily by increasing order volume and customer acquisition
- Long-term growth trends show sustained scaling after initial expansion
- Forecasting models project **2026 revenue to reach approximately $4.79M**
